About HH Construction Group Inc.

Raising the standard for quality-driven general construction services.

Who We Are

HH Construction Group Inc. is a full-service general construction company focused on clean work, strong structures and modern finishes. We handle residential and commercial projects from planning and permits all the way to the final punch list.

We know how stressful construction can be. That’s why we keep communication simple and honest, timelines realistic and job sites organized. Our goal is to make your experience with a general contractor in Los Angeles as smooth and transparent as possible.

Our Mission

Deliver high-quality construction with the same attention to detail we would want in our own homes and businesses — on time, with no surprises.

Core Values

  • Safety first on every site
  • Precise planning and execution
  • Honesty and transparency
  • Respect for clients and crews

Whether you are planning a new build, an ADU, an addition, or a full remodel, our team can walk you through design, permitting and construction from start to finish.

What We Do

  • New home construction
  • ADUs and additions
  • Kitchen and bathroom remodels
  • Commercial build-outs
  • Exterior upgrades and structural work

How We Work

We walk you through scope, budget and schedule before starting. During the project, you get updates, photos and clear next steps instead of surprises.
